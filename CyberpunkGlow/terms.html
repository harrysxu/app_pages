Last updated: May 10, 2026

Use of the App

Cyberpunk Glow is provided as a creative visual utility. You are responsible for using bright or flashing visuals safely and lawfully in your environment.

Glow Pro Purchases

Glow Pro unlocks advanced creation, sync, and export tools. Monthly and yearly plans are auto-renewable subscriptions. Glow Pro Lifetime is a non-consumable one-time purchase.

Billing and Renewal

Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. Auto-renewable subscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Your Apple ID account is charged by Apple according to App Store terms. You can manage or cancel subscriptions in App Store account settings.

Restore Purchases

The app provides Restore Purchases to refresh eligible App Store transactions on your device.

Feature Availability

Some features depend on device capability, system settings, permissions, local network conditions, thermal state, battery state, or App Store availability. HDR Boost, 120Hz, microphone analysis, Photos export, and Mosaic sync may not be available on every device or in every condition.

No Warranty

The app is provided as is, without warranties of uninterrupted availability, perfect visual alignment, or fitness for a particular event, venue, or production workflow.
