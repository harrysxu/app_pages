Bright and Flashing Visual Effects

Last updated: May 10, 2026

Cyberpunk Glow can display bright, high-contrast, animated, or flashing visuals. These effects may be uncomfortable or unsafe for some people, including people who are sensitive to flashing lights or have a history of photosensitive seizures, migraines, vertigo, or similar conditions.

Use Safely

  • Do not use strobe or flashing effects around people who may be sensitive to flashing lights.
  • Stop using the app immediately if you or someone nearby feels discomfort, dizziness, nausea, eye strain, headache, or disorientation.
  • Use lower brightness in dark rooms, vehicles, crowds, concerts, and shared spaces.
  • Follow venue rules and local laws when using bright screens in public.

Device and Display Limits

HDR Boost, peak brightness, color output, 120Hz motion, and thermal behavior depend on the device, display, battery, temperature, system settings, and current iOS conditions. The app cannot guarantee a specific brightness level or visual result on every device.

Mosaic Mode

Mosaic mode uses nearby local-network connections. Timing, alignment, and continuity can vary with device placement, model differences, permissions, and network conditions. Do not rely on Mosaic mode for safety-critical signaling.

明亮和闪烁视觉效果

更新日期：2026年5月10日

赛博霓光可以显示明亮、高对比、动态或闪烁的视觉效果。这些效果可能让部分人感到不适或存在风险，包括对闪光敏感、曾有光敏性癫痫、偏头痛、眩晕或类似情况的人群。

安全使用

  • 请勿在可能对闪光敏感的人群附近使用频闪或闪烁效果。
  • 如果你或附近的人出现不适、头晕、恶心、眼疲劳、头痛或方向感混乱，请立即停止使用。
  • 在暗室、车辆、人群、演唱会和共享空间中，请降低亮度。
  • 在公共场所使用明亮屏幕时，请遵守场馆规则和当地法律。

设备和显示限制

HDR 增强、峰值亮度、颜色输出、120Hz 动效和发热表现取决于设备、显示屏、电池、温度、系统设置和当前 iOS 状态。应用无法保证所有设备都达到特定亮度或视觉结果。

拼屏模式

拼屏模式使用附近本地网络连接。时序、对齐和连续性会受到设备摆放、机型差异、权限和网络状态影响。请勿将拼屏模式用于安全关键提示。
