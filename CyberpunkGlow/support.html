Please include your device model, iOS version, app version, the feature you were using, and a short description of the issue.

Common Checks

  • For Audio-Reactive, confirm microphone permission is allowed in iOS Settings.
  • For Mosaic, confirm Local Network permission is allowed and nearby devices are awake with the app open.
  • For saving exports, confirm Photos add permission is allowed.
  • For Glow Pro, use Restore Purchases after reinstalling or changing devices.
  • HDR Boost and 120Hz depend on supported hardware and current system conditions.
